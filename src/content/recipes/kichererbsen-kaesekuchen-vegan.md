- For the crust:
- <span class="qty" data-base="180.0">180</span> g graham crackers
- <span class="qty" data-base="113.0">113</span> g vegan butter (melted)
- For the filling:
- <span class="qty" data-base="300.0">300</span> g cashews
- <span class="qty" data-base="1.0">1</span> can (approximately <span class="qty" data-base="400.0">400</span> g) chickpeas, drained
- <span class="qty" data-base="22.0">22</span> ml vanilla extract
- <span class="qty" data-base="400.0">400</span> ml full-fat unsweetened coconut milk
- <span class="qty" data-base="120.0">120</span> ml maple syrup
- <span class="qty" data-base="32.0">32</span> g almond butter
- Zest of <span class="qty" data-base="2.0">2</span> lemons
- Large pinch of kosher salt
- <span class="qty" data-base="24.0">24</span> g cornstarch
- For the topping:
- Strawberries
- <span class="qty" data-base="2.0">2</span> clementines
- <span class="qty" data-base="60.0">60</span> ml maple syrup
- <span class="qty" data-base="1.25">1.25</span> ml vanilla extract
- Prepare the crust:
- Preheat oven to 175°C.
- Blend graham crackers into fine crumbs.
- Mix crumbs with melted vegan butter until well combined.
- Press mixture into a 9-inch springform pan lined with parchment paper, covering the bottom and slightly up the sides.
- Place crust in the freezer to firm up.
- Prepare the filling:
- Boil cashews in water with a pinch of salt for <span class="qty" data-base="15.0">15</span> minutes; drain.
- In a blender, combine drained chickpeas, vanilla extract, coconut milk, maple syrup, almond butter, lemon zest, and salt. Blend until smooth.
- Add boiled cashews to the blender and blend until creamy.
- Incorporate cornstarch and blend briefly to combine.
- Assemble and bake:
- Pour filling into the prepared crust.
- Bake for 38-<span class="qty" data-base="45.0">45</span> minutes, until the top is lightly browned.
- Remove from oven and refrigerate overnight to set.
- Prepare the topping:
- Juice clementines into a saucepan through a strainer.
- Quarter strawberries and add to the saucepan.
- Add maple syrup and vanilla extract.
- Bring mixture to a boil, stirring until strawberries break down and sauce thickens.
- Let cool, then add a pinch of salt and stir.
