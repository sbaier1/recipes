## Zutaten

- Von https://youtu.be/9ttJgoF9afo?si=l4Z439RdxvHglwex
- Custard for <span class="qty" data-base="1.0">1</span> log of dough (if baking both logs, double the recipe):
- <span class="qty" data-base="24.0">24</span>g ap flour
- Pinch of salt
- <span class="qty" data-base="100.0">100</span>g milk
- <span class="qty" data-base="80.0">80</span>g cream
- A few strips of lemon zest removed with a peeler
- <span class="qty" data-base="75.0">75</span>g maple syrup (from the fridge)
- <span class="qty" data-base="0.5">1/2</span> tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of cinnamon
- <span class="qty" data-base="1.0">1</span> tsp pomegranate molasses
- <span class="qty" data-base="3.0">3</span> yolks
- <span class="qty" data-base="1.0">1</span> plum, sliced paper thin (optional)
- Put the flour and salt into a bowl.  Put the milk, cream and zest into a pyrex cup and microwave until boiling.  Add enough milk mixture to the flour to create a thick paste (about half of milk mixture).  Whisk until no lumps remain.  Slowly add the rest.  Add maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon, pomegranate molasses, and whisk to combine.  Cool to warm.  Whisk in the yolks.  Strain through a fine mesh sieve.  Can be covered and refrigerated for a few hours if you can’t bake right away.
- 260c 12min
