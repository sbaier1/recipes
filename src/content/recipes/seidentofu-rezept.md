## Zutaten

- For the soy milk:
- <span class="qty" data-base="250.0">250</span>g dried soybeans (soaked overnight in at least <span class="qty" data-base="600.0">600</span>mL of water)
- <span class="qty" data-base="2.5">2.5</span>L water For coagulating into tofu: -
- <span class="qty" data-base="7.5">7.5</span>g GDL (glucono delta-lactone, tofu coagulant) -
- <span class="qty" data-base="2.0">2</span> tbsp water
- 2️⃣ Cook the soy milk: Heat the strained soy milk in a pot over medium-high, stirring often. Bring to a boil, then simmer for <span class="qty" data-base="10.0">10</span> minutes. Remove from heat.
- 3️⃣ Prepare the GDL: In a heatproof bowl, dissolve <span class="qty" data-base="7.5">7.5</span>g of GDL in <span class="qty" data-base="2.0">2</span> tbsp water.
- 4️⃣ Coagulate: Let the soy milk cool to 80°C. Pour from a height into the GDL mixture for even distribution. (Optional: skim bubbles.)
- 5️⃣ Set: Cover the bowl and let it rest undisturbed for <span class="qty" data-base="30.0">30</span> minutes until soft and silky.
- ratio:
- GDL = 0,0027 * sojamilch gewicht, ggf bissl mehr
