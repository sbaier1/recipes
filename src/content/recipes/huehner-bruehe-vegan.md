## Zutaten

- <span class="qty" data-base="30">30</span> g Glutamat
- <span class="qty" data-base="50">50</span> g Dextrose
- <span class="qty" data-base="40">40</span> g Salz
- <span class="qty" data-base="1">1</span> g Cystein
- <span class="qty" data-base="1">1</span> g Taurin
- <span class="qty" data-base="8">8</span> g Methionin
- <span class="qty" data-base="30">30</span> g Sojaprotein
- <span class="qty" data-base="10">10</span> g Mehl
- <span class="qty" data-base="10">10</span> g Leinöl
- <span class="qty" data-base="2">2</span> g Maggi
- <span class="qty" data-base="1">1</span> g Kombupulver
- <span class="qty" data-base="5">5</span> g Hefe- oder B12-Flocken
- <span class="qty" data-base="1">1</span> g Pilzpulver
- Gewürze (Knoblauch, Zwiebel, weißer Pfeffer)

## Zubereitung

- [ ] Alle Zutaten vermengen.
- [ ] Für Rinderbrühe: Cystein weglassen und dafür 1:1 Taurin und Thiamin (B1) sowie Miso und Sojasauce hinzufügen.
- [ ] Anwendung: <span class="qty" data-base="1">1</span> EL pro <span class="qty" data-base="250">250</span> ml Wasser.
